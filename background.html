PASTIL

Chicken Pastil is a beloved Filipino delicacy that tantalizes taste buds with its irresistible combination of tender chicken, aromatic spices, and fragrant banana leaves. This traditional dish embodies the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines, delivering a symphony of flavors in every bite. Marinated to perfection and steamed to retain its juiciness, Chicken Pastil is a culinary masterpiece that promises to delight even the most discerning palates. Whether enjoyed as a hearty meal or a savory snack, this dish is sure to transport you to the bustling streets and vibrant markets of the Philippines with its authentic taste and cultural charm.
